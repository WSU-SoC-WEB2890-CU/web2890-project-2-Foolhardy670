Coach Don Hickman

Don and his wife Stacey have been married for over 34 years. They started at the bottom and climbed to the top, with a lot of life lessons learned along the way. Stacey often says: "If only we had actually budgeted all those years ago."

They are passionate about helping others succeed with their money and achieve their life's purpose. There is nothing secret about their success with money. They communicate, they budget, they have zero debt (including the house), and they saved for retirement and their kid's education. They're in Dave Ramsey's Baby Step 7 - they are living and giving the life they want.
