Our Services

Individual / Couples Financial Coaching

Are you looking for someone who can help you get your money and finances under control? Are you looking for someone who can help you successfully plan, set goals, and take control of your future? Or maybe, you are doing "OK" and just need a "look under the hood" to make sure? You've come to the right place.

Coach Don Hickman has helped hundreds of people just like you turn their personal financial stress into personal financial wellness.

Employer-based Financial Literacy Training

Employees who suffer from personal financial stress are anxious, depressed, and do not know what to do. They are 10 times more likely to not finish tasks at work and two times more likely to be looking for a new job. They are costing you in lost productivity and increased health care costs.

Coach Don Hickman provides employee financial literacy training classes for companies in northern Utah (and Idaho and Wyoming too!)

Small Business Financial Coaching

Does your American Dream sometimes feel like an American Nightmare? Successfully running a small business is hard. Making payroll can be hard. Planning for future growth is vital, yet sometimes you just don't see how you will be able to afford it. If you are you a 1099 in the Gig Economy, do you know how to create and manage your profit / loss statements and properly pay your estimated taxes?

As a small businessman himself, Coach Don Hickman brings his years of experience to you and helps you get control of your company finances.
